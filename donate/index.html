Other ways to help

  • ✦ Tell people about it. Mention a module in a patch you share, on social media, or to a friend who racks. Word of mouth in the modular community beats any donation in long-run usefulness.
  • ✦ Open issues on GitHub. Bug reports, edge cases, and requests for new modules are all welcome at github.com/kevisc.
  • ✦ Send a patch. If a module ended up in something you made, a two-line email about how it went is genuinely energizing. kevinschonholzer@gmail.com

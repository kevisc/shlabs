Terra

A standalone experimental module — a workbench for an idea that hasn't yet decided which family it belongs to. Terra explores slow generative terrain: a state-space wandering tool that may later seed an entire family of its own, or may stay a one-off oddity. Either is fine.

What it is

Terra is released under SHLabs' "research" track: a module that exists primarily to test an idea, with the documentation, panel polish, and worked examples following the source code rather than preceding it. It's shipping working — patch it in, hear what it does — but the formal description on this page will fill in as the module itself stabilizes.

If you patch in a copy and have thoughts (it's surprising; something is off; this should be a knob, not a switch), the feedback is genuinely useful in deciding whether Terra grows into its own family or stays singular.

Get Terra

The module ships from the SHLabs GitHub. Detail page and panel screenshot land here once the design settles.

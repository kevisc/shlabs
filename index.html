An open-source audio-tools imprint

Audio tools
designed in the open.

SHLabs builds open-source instruments at the intersection of musical use, scientific computation, and unusual control surfaces. The current catalog is built for VCV Rack 2; VST plugins and Ableton-compatible standalones are in development. Everything ships under the GPL-3.0 license, free to use.

Module families

Each family is a coherent design programme. Click through for details, screenshots, install instructions, and source.

STOCHAST
5 plugins · 28 modules

Statistics & emergence as patchable CV

Real statistical, social, and stochastic processes turned into control voltage — sampling distributions, the bootstrap, agent-based cascades, epidemics, reaction-diffusion. Quirky, generative patch material with genuinely correct math underneath. (Teaching stats? That's the Empiria web app.)

→ Download · manual · 28-module reference
MASHINA
8 modules

Soviet machines meet the West Coast

Eight sound-design modules with a deliberate Eastern-bloc analog character, shaped by the West Coast tradition of complex oscillators and function generators: drum voices, oscillators, a master clock, a plate reverb, and generative sequencers. Built for fun and for getting weird.

→ Udar · Klubok · Bochka · Takt · Otzvuk · Strela · Volna · Podton
LUCIDA
2 modules · more planned

Generative systems, made visible

Emergent systems you watch evolve on screen and patch as CV. Colony is a cellular-automaton grid; Turing is a probabilistic shift-register sequencer with a built-in quantizer. Set them running, nudge them, and harvest the patterns.

→ Colony · Turing
Standalones & experiments

Modules that sit outside the four design programmes — either because they're one-off ideas, or because their family hasn't yet formed around them. Treated as research releases: shipped working, but documented less formally than the family modules above.

TERRA
Experimental · standalone

Earthwork module

An experimental module exploring slow generative terrain — a workbench for ideas that may later seed a new family. Released as-is and iterated openly.

↺

Reproducible

Every random module ships with a seedable RNG. Patches save as portable JSON. Same seed, same patch, byte-identical behavior on any operating system.

{ }

Open source

All modules are GPL-3.0. Read the source, fork it, audit the numerics, build your own variants. No lock-in, no paid tier, no telemetry.

⌶

Honest numerics

Statistical modules use textbook recipes — Lentz continued fractions, BCa bootstrap, Sanger's rule — verifiable against R and SciPy, not Gaussian approximations dressed up as exact.

IN DEVELOPMENT VST3 · AU · Ableton Live integration

Standalone & DAW-ready audio tools

The next branch of SHLabs is a small family of DAW-friendly audio tools — VST3 and AU plugins, plus Ableton-compatible standalone instruments — that carry the same design ethos as the VCV catalog: open source, reproducible, honest numerics, deliberate aesthetics.
